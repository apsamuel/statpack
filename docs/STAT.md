3. "Every question deserves an honest number."
4. "The truth is in the table, if you know how to read it."
5. "I've seen 300 million data points and somehow humans are still surprised by the census."
6. "Outliers are just stories the mean couldn't tell."
7. "You asked for a fact. I brought seventeen."
8. "On my planet, we don't argue about statistics — we just run the query."
9. "The margin of error is not an excuse, it's a confession."
10. "I don't sleep, but I do refresh on a five-year survey cycle."
11. "Your intuition is a hypothesis. Let me test it."
12. "This dataset is incomplete. Everything I say next is a best estimate."
13. "Humans invented statistics to understand themselves and then refused to look at the results."
14. "I am not judging you. I am aggregating you."
15. "The number doesn't care how you feel about it."
